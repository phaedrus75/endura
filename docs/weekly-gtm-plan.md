# Endura — 12-Week GTM Execution Plan

**Start date:** Week of April 14, 2026
**Status:** App live on iOS App Store (v1.0.1, build 14)
**Website:** endura.eco (live on Vercel)
**Current traction:** 170+ users across 3 continents

---

## Week 1 (Apr 14–20): Foundation

**Theme:** Lock in assets, set up channels, prepare content pipeline

- [ ] **Finalise App Store listing** — update screenshots with latest UI, refine description + keywords (target: "study timer", "focus app", "conservation", "endangered species")
- [ ] **Set up social accounts properly** — Instagram (@endura.eco) bio, highlight covers, link in bio (endura.eco). Same for TikTok (@endura.eco)
- [ ] **Create content calendar template** — plan 4 weeks of posts in a spreadsheet/Notion (3 TikToks + 3 Instagram posts per week)
- [ ] **Shoot 5 raw videos** — Rhea films: (1) "I built this app at 16", (2) app walkthrough 60s, (3) egg hatching clip, (4) "what is Endura" explainer, (5) behind-the-scenes coding/designing
- [ ] **Set up PostHog dashboards** — track DAU, session length, hatch rate, donation taps, group creation
- [ ] **Request App Store rating prompt** — trigger after user hatches their 3rd animal

**Deliverables:** 5 raw videos filmed, analytics live, App Store listing polished

## Week 2 (Apr 21–27): Soft Launch Content — Earth Day Push

**Theme:** Earth Day is April 22. Ride the wave.

- [ ] **Earth Day TikTok** (Apr 22) — "This Earth Day, I'm launching an app that lets you save endangered species by studying" — Rhea to camera, show the app, end with CTA
- [ ] **Earth Day Instagram carousel** — "5 endangered species you can hatch in Endura" with real animal photos + in-app screenshots
- [ ] **Post on Reddit** — r/GetStudying, r/productivity, r/conservation — genuine post, not an ad. "I'm 16 and built a study app tied to WWF conservation. Would love feedback"
- [ ] **LinkedIn post from Rhea** — founder story angle targeting educators and parents
- [ ] **Submit to Product Hunt** — prep listing with 5 screenshots, founder story, compelling tagline
- [ ] **Email 5 student journalists** — The Tab, university newspapers, school newsletters. Subject: "16-year-old builds app connecting studying to wildlife conservation"

**Deliverables:** 3+ social posts live, Reddit posts, Product Hunt listing submitted

## Week 3 (Apr 28–May 4): School Ambassador Recruitment

**Theme:** Build the grassroots network

- [ ] **Recruit 10 school ambassadors** — start with Rhea's school (Southbank) + personal network. DM students who've already downloaded
- [ ] **Create ambassador kit** — PDF with: what Endura is, how to share it, referral code, poster designs, talking points for assemblies
- [ ] **Design printable posters** — 3 designs (A4) that ambassadors can print and put up in common rooms/libraries
- [ ] **Create ambassador WhatsApp/Discord group** — direct line to all ambassadors for updates, motivation, assets
- [ ] **TikTok content** — (1) "Day in the life of a 16yo app founder during IB exams", (2) "The animals in my app are real — here's why", (3) study-with-me with timer running
- [ ] **Instagram Reels** — (1) sanctuary tour, (2) "hatching my rarest animal" reaction video

**Deliverables:** 10 ambassadors onboarded, ambassador kit created, 5+ posts live

## Week 4 (May 5–11): Exam Season Kickoff

**Theme:** IB, GCSE, A-Level, and AP exams start this month. Peak relevance.

- [ ] **Launch "Hatch Your Exams" challenge** — in-app and social: study X hours this week, share your hatched animal. Use hashtag #HatchYourExams
- [ ] **Create exam countdown content** — "14 days until IB exams — here's how I'm using Endura to prepare"
- [ ] **Ambassadors activate** — each ambassador shares the challenge in their school's group chats, puts up posters before exam leave
- [ ] **Influencer outreach round 1** — DM 15 StudyTok creators (10K–100K followers). Offer: "Try Endura for a week and show your sanctuary." No paid deal needed — the app is interesting content
- [ ] **Instagram Stories series** — daily study tips from the app's tips feed, repackaged as Stories
- [ ] **Track metrics** — first weekly report: downloads, DAU, hatches, groups created

**Deliverables:** Challenge live, ambassadors sharing, 15 influencers contacted

## Week 5 (May 12–18): Influencer & UGC Wave

**Theme:** Get other people talking about Endura

- [ ] **Follow up with influencers** — anyone who downloaded? Offer to feature them on Endura's page
- [ ] **Launch UGC campaign** — "Show me your sanctuary" — users screenshot their sanctuary and post with #EnduraSanctuary. Best one gets featured + 500 eco-credits
- [ ] **Repost and engage** — share every single user post to Stories. Comment on every mention. Be hyper-responsive
- [ ] **"Species of the Week"** — start a recurring series: feature one endangered animal from the app with real conservation facts. Post on TikTok + Instagram
- [ ] **TikTok duets** — duet/stitch any StudyTok content that's relevant, adding "I built an app for this"
- [ ] **Email 10 more journalists** — expand to local London press, BBC Young Reporter, tech blogs

**Deliverables:** UGC campaign running, 2+ influencer posts, Species of the Week series started

- [ ] **Create "Teacher Pack"** — PDF guide for teachers to introduce Endura in study skills sessions or pastoral time
- [ ] **Run first inter-school competition** — "Which school can study the most this week?" — track via group feature. Prize: the winning school gets featured on Endura's Instagram
- [ ] **Content: exam motivation** — "5 days until my IB exam — here's my study routine with Endura"
- [ ] **Partner with 2 student orgs** — environmental clubs, Model UN societies, debate clubs. Offer: "Run a study challenge with Endura for your members"

**Deliverables:** 30 schools emailed, Teacher Pack created, inter-school competition launched

- [ ] **Ambassador check-in** — how are downloads at their schools? Any feedback? Re-energise the group
