## FEATURES

- **Section Badge:** Why Endura?
- **Section Headline:** Studying that actually / makes a difference
- **Section Subheadline:** Every feature is designed to keep you focused, motivated, and connected to something bigger than grades.

### Feature Cards

1. **⏳ Gamified Study Timer**
   Set focused study sessions with our beautiful timer. Pick your subject, start the clock, and watch your egg grow closer to hatching with every minute you study.

2. **🐣 Hatch Endangered Animals**
   Complete study sessions to unlock an interactive hatching experience — tap to crack the egg and reveal one of 30+ real endangered species. Collect, nickname, and learn about each one.

3. **📋 Subjects & Task Management**
   Organise your study by subject, create to-dos with due dates, and track what needs doing. Everything in one place so you can focus on what matters.

4. **👥 Study With Friends**
   Join groups, set group goals by subject, compete on weekly and all-time leaderboards, and react to friends' achievements. Studying is better together.

5. **🏡 Sanctuary & Shop**
   Build a beautiful habitat for your animals. Spend eco-credits in the shop on accessories and decorations. Make your sanctuary uniquely yours.

6. **🏅 Badges & Study Tips**
   Earn 50+ badges as you study and protect wildlife. Discover community-powered study tips with voting and sharing to help you and your friends study smarter.

## HOW IT WORKS

- **Section Badge:** Simple as 1-2-3
- **Section Headline:** How it works

### Steps

1. **📚 Study** (Step 01)
   Choose from your subjects, set a timer, and focus. Our distraction-free timer keeps you on track with gentle motivation — leave early and your egg might not make it!

2. **🐣 Hatch** (Step 02)
   Complete your session and tap to crack open your egg in an interactive hatching experience. Discover one of 30+ real endangered species, learn about its conservation status, and earn eco-credits.

3. **🌍 Protect** (Step 03)
   Your study hours translate to real impact. Donate directly to WWF from inside the app — no middlemen, no detours. The more you study, the more species you help save.

- **Mobile flow text:** study → hatch → protect
